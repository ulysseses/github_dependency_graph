Repo Flower

The top K=8 of the most depended-on (i.e. popular) repos are chosen. Then, for each of these repos, only N=8 of the repo's dependees are shown, ranked by how many other repos depend on it (popularity). Then, for each of these repos, find its top N=8 most popular repos.
